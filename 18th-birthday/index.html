Guys I'm 18

Dylan Lu

Dec 7, 2023 • 1 min read

Hey, I'm 18 now. I just bought a $2 lottery scratcher and won $40. And Sophia made me a cake. Things are pretty good so far!

Anyway, since I haven't posted recently (due to college apps), I'll just do a quick update with everything that happened recently.

I got the Logitech G Pro X 2 headsets to go with my new game, Elden Ring. I've been playing it way too much, especially since I have more important stuff to do.

I'm ditching the Gunn traffic idea to build a game: Gunn Football! It's like football... except you shoot your opponent with a gun. Pretty genius if you ask me.

Beat Saber just dropped OST 6 so I'm really excited about that. The first two were two easy, but the last two are literally impossible, so I'll have to practice a lot to beat them.

Basketball's going well but making my layups/threes is the only thing holding me back from 8+ points per game. I get like 8-10 mins of playing time, which is not terrible I guess.

Got a new phone like last month!

Should be fine on grades, especially now that Physics grades are finally out and Econ is giving lots of extra credit.

Working on my Stanford and Princeton essays right now. Bro, Sunita literally did not add the Princeton engineering essay so I wouldn't have written it if I didn't see Max's computer during PRIME. I'll do Carnegie Mellon after since it has a nice 20% acceptance rate if I do a specific college.

That's basically it, but it's kind of weird that I'll never be a kid again.
