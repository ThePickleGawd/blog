Two Days Back In

Day two. Lot's of cool things happening

Dylan Lu

Sep 14, 2023 • 1 min read

I'm back, and I'm feeling great. No more being lazy, no more being scared to take calls, no more avoiding people. Today, after my college counselor meeting with Ms. Ross, I called up Eric from Vypin. He suggested that we partner and use his prebuilt Bluetooth solution. My initial thoughts are that it could be good, but I'm not so sure about how expensive it will be, how long the battery will last, and how big it will be. In these aspects, I think my design wins, but it clearly is not reliable.

And, I finally sent an email to Dave asking for a recommendation letter, something I've put off for almost 2 months now (I have zero confidence in myself sometimes). But I'm back, and I'm feeling great.
